Learning Outcome Domains

Conceptual
Design thinking + communication theory
MLO 1 (Design Thinking) and MLO 3 (User-Centered Design) — foundational problem-solving and audience awareness.
Technical
Tools, typography, UI/UX
MLO 2 (Tools & Tech) and MLO 4 (UX/UI Design) — software fluency and interface design skills.
Applied
Real-world problem solving, data vis
MLO 5 (Data Visualization) — translating complex information into clear, ethical visual narratives.
Professional
Portfolio, freelancing, teamwork
MLO 6 (Portfolio), MLO 7 (Collaboration), MLO 8 (Entrepreneurship) — career-readiness skills.

Key Design Highlights

All MLOs covered — no gaps across the curriculum
MLO 1 & MLO 6 heavily scaffolded — design thinking and portfolio run throughout
Data visualization intentional — built into DSGN 107, 310, 320, 498, 410
AI integration threaded — ethical, strategic use in every course
Experiential learning — internship and capstone as high-impact finishers
