## What the source actually says

**Purpose** (verbatim from `source/program-data.md`):

> Consumer product design — structural packaging (folds, dielines, mockups), brand identity in 3D space, materials/sustainability/printing considerations, visual storytelling and shelf impact, regulatory and ethical considerations, and market positioning. Prerequisites: DSGN 114 and 205.

That is the entirety of the course-design guidance in the source. The
document describes **what** the course covers, never **how**, **in what
sequence**, **with what tools**, or **measured against what rubrics**.
