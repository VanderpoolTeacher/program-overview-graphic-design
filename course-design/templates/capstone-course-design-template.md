## Next steps

This document is the spec. To produce the course materials:

1. **Author the assignments.** Use
   `course-design/templates/assignment-template.md`. Each assignment
   declares the CLOs it addresses (which must be a subset of those
   listed at the top of *this* document) and includes a rubric whose
   criteria align with this document's course-level rubric.
2. **Build the weekly schedule.** Map the course arc above to the
   actual academic calendar.
3. **Validate.** Run
   `./scripts/validate-course-design.sh {{COURSE_CODE}}` after
   assignments are authored.
4. **Update progress tracker.** Advance the course's stage in
   `course-design/progress.html` to reflect work done.
