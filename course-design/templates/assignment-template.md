## AI integration

> Required block. AI must be a *tool* in the work, not a footnote about
> the work. Pick one of these patterns:
>
> - **Co-author:** AI helps draft / edit / brainstorm; student keeps
>   final say.
> - **Operations:** AI handles a repeatable task (estimating, summarizing
>   research, formatting).
> - **Critic:** AI reviews student's draft and surfaces gaps.
>
> Always require students to disclose **what** they used and **how** they
> verified outputs. Do not let "I used ChatGPT" stand on its own.
